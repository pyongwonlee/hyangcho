, BLANK BOOK
2015, collected paper
I notice that some books, especially old paperbacks, contain one or two blank papers. Unlike the flyleaf or end paper which connects the cover and the body of the book, these empty pages does not seem to have any function. I have collected these blank papers by cutting them carefully without damaging the books and bound them together making a book of its own.
