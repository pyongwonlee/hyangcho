BOOKS 1
2010, found books, glue
BOOKS 1 consists of various genre fictions discarded by public libraries. I glued all the pages of each book. In so doing, the stories remain out of reach and the familiar books become unfamiliar objects with a strange tactility. There are more than 80 books in this series.
