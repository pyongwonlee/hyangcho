JARS
2015, digital images of 61 glass jars
JARS is a 61-minute digital slide show of 61 images of mass produced clear glass jars from various food products. I have collected as many different clear glass jars as possible. After consuming the content, I de-labelled, cleaned, and photographed each jar.
JARS is presented as a digital slideshow with each jar image shown for one minute duration.
