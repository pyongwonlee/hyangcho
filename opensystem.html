OPEN SYSTEM
2006, Installation: house paint, egg flats, Styrofoam, paint lids
OPEN SYSTEM consists of painted and stacked egg flats, the Styrofoam block, and stacked paint can lids. The system bridging the three works was developed from the close observation of the material. As the stack of painted egg flats grew, the coloured dots on the Styrofoam expanded, and the pile of paint can lids became taller.
