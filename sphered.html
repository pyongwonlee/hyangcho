SPHERED
2016, found glass
SPHERED is casted with broken shards from the collected clear glass jars, which I used for the previous work "Jars."
After documenting the clear glass jars, I started to break them into small pieces and had them casted into a sphere.
Due to the different chemical compounds of each jar, the shards are incompatible to one another. There is a possibility of disintegrating through the time. It can be cracking, flaking, or splintering at any time. Or it can last forever.
With all the shards from the clear glass jars, I was able to produce 4 of them. Each one is about 6.5 inch diameter and weigh about 11.5 pounds.

Fabricated by Kasia Czarnota
