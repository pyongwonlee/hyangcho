SPHERED
2016, found glass
SPHERED is casted with broken shards from the collected clear jars that I used for the previous work "Jars." After documenting the clear jars, I started to break them into small pieces and had them casted into a sphere. Due to the different chemical compounds of each jar, the shards are incompatible to one another. There is a possibility of disintegrating through the time. It can be cracking, flaking, splintering, or it can last forever. This unpredictable future is the essential nature of the work.

With all the shards from the clear glass jars, I was able to produce 4 of them. Each one is about 6.5 inch diameter and weigh about 11.5 pounds.

Fabricated by Kasia Czarnota
