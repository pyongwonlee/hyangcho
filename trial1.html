TRIAL 1
2012, pencil on paper
I reproduced part of Kafka's original manuscript of "The Trial", chapter 9, "Im Dom" written in German, which I found on the Internet. There are 26 pages of manuscript. I printed them out and traced them in pencil on a loose leaf sketchbook.
I made three versions of 26 drawings of the manuscripts and compiled them into unbound books. Each book is supposed to be identical but obliged to be different due to my hand copying.
