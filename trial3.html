TRIAL 3
2013, typing on paper
TRIAL 3 is a series of transcription of the audio book of "The Trial" by Franz Kafka, made on an electronic typewriter. The speed of my typing is unable to keep up with the speed of the reading in the audio book. Therefore, the letters and words are missing and the sentences are broken.
I repeated the process of transcription of entire book and produced 8 versions of TRIAL 3.
